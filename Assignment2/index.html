Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Introduction:

    The tutorial introduces the "Berry Bunch Handheld Windmill Fan" craft project.

  2. Materials Needed:
    • Paper
    • Scissors
    • Straw or wooden stick
    • Push pin or thumbtack
    • Glue or tape
    • Markers or crayons (optional for decoration)
  3. Step 1: Cut the Paper

    Start with a square piece of paper. Fold the paper diagonally in half to form a triangle. Fold the triangle in half again to form a smaller triangle. Unfold the paper back to the square shape. The creases will form an X.

  4. Step 2: Make the Cuts

    Using scissors, cut along the creases starting from each corner of the square towards the center, stopping about halfway to the center point. Do not cut all the way through.

  5. Step 3: Form the Windmill Blades

    Take one corner of each cut section and fold it towards the center. Do this for every other corner (four corners in total). Secure the corners in the center with glue, tape, or by using a push pin.

  6. Step 4: Attach to the Stick

    Once all four corners are secured in the center, use a push pin or thumbtack to attach the paper windmill to the end of a straw or wooden stick. Ensure the pin or thumbtack is not too tight, so the windmill can spin freely.

  7. Step 5: Optional Decoration

    Use markers or crayons to decorate the windmill as desired. This step is optional but adds a personal touch to your craft.

  8. Conclusion:

    The tutorial concludes with a demonstration of the finished handheld windmill fan. Spin it gently to see it in action!
